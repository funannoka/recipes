Ingredients:
 - 3/4 cups sugar
 - 2 cups all-purpose flour
 - 2 dashes of cinammon
 - (optional for fluffy) 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda 
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 4 eggs
 - 2 cup milk
 - 1/2 cup butter
 - 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - vegetable oil (for frying)

Directions:
 1. Pour some oil to pan and set the stove to medium low.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cinammon, baking soda and  	 	salt. Add eggs, milk and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in melted butter. Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 3. Pour some batter into oiled pan, wait 2 mins or before it overcooks and then flip the cake to the other side. Repeat, adding a bit of oil to the pan for each pancake until batter is finished. Enjoy with some eggs and avocado.
